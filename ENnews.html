Volkswagen ID. Buzz: Sustainable, with a lot of attention to detail


Two tones, with a lot of attention to detail.

ID. Buzz will be available in seven plain colors and four in two shades. The colors match the exterior paint and are optionally reflected in the seat upholstery, dashboard and interior door trim. Ambient lighting is available as an option with up to 30 shades. The attention to detail in the new model is also evident in a number of ID reasons. Buzz that decorates the interior in various places. The vast majority are discovered at a glance - even functional and smart features such as a bottle opener and a windshield squeegee.



The new Polo GTI. A sports car, in the purest tradition


The heart of a GTI is always the engine. Like all its predecessors, the new Polo GTI always has front-wheel drive. The direct-injection gasoline turbo engine shifts gears with a seven-speed dual-clutch (DSG) transmission, which is standard. The main components of the turbo engine in the new Polo GTI, which is mounted transversely to the front of the car, are features such as a dual injection system with direct combined injection and in the fuel gallery, an engine control unit with four main processors and an electronic valve lift system on the intake side. Although it has a high efficiency, the engine is impressively efficient with moderate fuel consumption. The light weight of the new Polo GTI contributes to this.



The new Volkswagen T-Roc: expressive design and innovative technology


The combination of a progressive look, sporty performance and unmatched versatility have made the T-Roc a popular car, extremely well sold over the last four years. Without a direct predecessor, this model overnight became a mainstay of the Volkswagen product range. Since its launch in 2017, more than one million units of this compact SUV have been sold. Since the end of 2019, the range of models has included the extremely sporty version of the T-Roc R. Since the spring of 2020, the T-Roc Cabriolet has combined the strengths of an SUV with the option to drive with the roof lowered.



The performance of the new Golf R1 sets new standards


Featuring all-wheel drive and the most exclusive design and the most exclusive equipment, this extremely dynamic car represents the top standard of technology in its class - Golf class. And this time things are no different: Golf R is the first Volkswagen launched with a completely new all-wheel drive system: 4MOTION with R-Performance torque vector control. This system, intelligently interconnected by the vehicle's dynamic settings, makes new dimensions of sportiness possible.
