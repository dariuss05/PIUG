The new T-Roc

Expressive design and innovative technology



Wolfsburg, Germany - world premiere of the new T-Roc: With a fresh design, innovative support systems and numerous digital services, Volkswagen continues the success story of its compact crossover model. An improved exterior defines the unmistakable look of the T-Roc, T-Roc R and T Roc Cabriolet models. The interior has been refined with high quality materials, a redesigned infotainment display and digital cockpit as standard. Innovative high-end technology has now been integrated into the family of compact SUV models - in the form of IQ.Light, with LED matrix headlights and IQ.Drive assistance systems with Travel Assist, as well as the ACC predictive cruise control. In this way, Volkswagen is making great strides towards becoming a software-oriented mobility provider as part of its ACCELERATED strategy.

The combination of a progressive look, sporty performance and unmatched versatility have made the T-Roc a popular car, extremely well sold over the last four years. Without a direct predecessor, this model overnight became a mainstay of the Volkswagen product range. Since its launch in 2017, more than one million units of this compact SUV have been sold. Since the end of 2019, the range of models has included the extremely sporty version of the T-Roc R. Since the spring of 2020, the T-Roc Cabriolet has combined the strengths of an SUV with the option to drive with the roof lowered.

Sophisticated interior, improved exterior design

With a foam surface and distinctive joints, the new dashboard brings modern design elements and the highest quality inside the new T-Roc. The Infotainment display is now designed as a tablet and positioned on the dashboard in the same field of view as the dashboard behind the steering wheel. Depending on the equipment, the Infotainment display measures between 6.5 inches and 9.2 inches. The compact SUV is equipped as standard with a digital dashboard, optionally available with a size of up to 10.25 inches, in the form of the digital cockpit Pro. The multifunction steering wheel, also part of the standard package, has a new design that contributes to the intuitive control of the vehicle.

The high-quality textile upholstery of the doors is now standard. For the Style and R-Line equipment lines, the door and armrest upholstery is made of eco-leather and has designer stems, just like the headrests. Another feature of the Style line of equipment is the center panels of the ArtVelours comfort seats. In combination with the R-Line, state-of-the-art sports seats are available as an option for both the driver and front passenger (previously only available on the T Roc R), as well as Nappa leather upholstery.
br The lines of the new exterior of the T-Roc model have been improved, making the unmistakable crossover design all the more striking. LED headlights and dark taillights are included as standard. IQ LIGHT with LED matrix headlights and an illuminated radiator grille strip, as well as rear position lights with a new graphic structure and dynamic turn signals are available on request. Five new exterior paint colors and 17- and 19-inch alloy wheels with a new design give customers even more opportunities to customize their car.

Below you will find the additional features compared to the previous model for each equipment line. At the same time, each feature displayed comes in addition to the lower equipment line.

New levels of digitization and connectivity

The new T-Roc features advanced Front Assist and Lane Assist as standard. Moreover, the new IQ.DRIVE Travel Assist and predictive cruise control allow assisted steering, braking and acceleration control at speeds of up to 210 km / h. By integrating signals from the front camera as well as GPS and map data, the vehicle control system can include information on local speed limits, location markers, intersections and roundabouts.

The new T-Roc has a third generation Modular Infotainment Toolkit (MIB3). This allows the use of a wide range of online features and services. We Connect Plus services can be used free of charge in Europe for one year as part of the standard package. This means that natural online voice command and streaming services are available. Apple CarPlay ™ and Android Auto ™ functions can also be integrated wirelessly into the control system via App-Connect Wireless, if desired.

TSI and TDI efficient engines, 7-speed and 4MOTION DSG gearboxes available as an option

In the new engine portfolio of the new T-Roc, there are three TSI engines and two TDI engines to choose from. Depending on the engine chosen, a 6-speed manual gearbox or a 7-speed DSG gearbox drives the front axle. The 1.0-liter three-cylinder TSI engine delivers a maximum power of 81 kW (110 hp) 1. The four-cylinder engines generate 110 kW (150 hp) in the 1.5-liter TSI version and 140 kW (190 hp) in the 2.0-liter TSI version. The range is completed by two 2.0-liter TDI engines that offer 85 kW (115 hp) and 110 kW (150 hp), respectively. As an alternative to front-wheel drive, 4MOTION all-wheel drive in combination with the 110 kW (150 hp) diesel engine ensures superior power transmission. The 2.0-liter, 140 hp (190 hp) TSI engine is combined with 4MOTION all-wheel drive as standard.

Available in a basic version and three other preconfigured equipment lines

The T-Roc model can be more easily adapted to individual tastes, thanks to the reorganization of the equipment lines in a Y structure.

The Style equipment line features two-tone paint, extra chrome elements, ambient lighting and comfort seats inside. The exterior design and equipment for the R-Line are now based to an even greater extent on the top-of-the-line T-Roc R model. with touch controls. In addition, the new T-Roc in combination with the R-Line has a sports package that includes the selection of travel profiles, progressive steering and sports running gear. Furthermore, customers can opt for a Black Style design package in combination with the Style and R-Line equipment lines. Its exterior design elements create a subtle sporty look.

The new T-Roc R: versatile performance

With its 221 kW (300 hp) 5 four-cylinder engine, the new T-Roc R is the most dynamic compact SUV in the model family. The standard sports undercarriage and 4MOTION all-wheel drive also contribute to this. In addition to the distinctive sound, the sporty performance is accentuated both visually inside and outside the R-specific exterior.
